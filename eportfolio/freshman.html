World History

This project was one of the most interesting projects done this year amongst the many history projects. Finished on Feburary 14, 2017 in my group of three people, this was a parody of a country song on the topic of communism during the age of the Industrial Revolution. We had to hold a meeting at my house specifically to finish writing the lyrics of the song and finally record it. Instead of using a karaoke accompaniment, I played a few keys on the piano, so as to allow the actual singing to be heard since we were all very quiet. While I wrote only a few of the actual lyrics, I did find information that we could incorporate into the song. I also recorded and edited the song. If I had to do something differently, however, I would have attempted to make the recording of our voices louder.
